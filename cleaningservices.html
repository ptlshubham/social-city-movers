Cleaning Services

Our Home Cleaning Service Will Make it appear as if you were Never There.

Moving is stressful enough without cleaning up after the move is complete. Social City Movers is here to alleviate the stress of moving. The last thing you need to think about is how to tidy up your house or apartment to prepare it for the new landlords or owners. We provide complete move-in and move-out assistance that allows you to concentrate on the most important thing - your new home.

Our professional cleaners ensure that no area of your apartment or home is left unclean. Utilizing the most advanced methods and techniques, we'll ensure that your place looks better than when you first moved into it.

Service that is convenient for Tenants & Landlords

Suppose you're a tenant looking to recover refunds on your security deposit or an owner who wants to speedily get a new tenant in an open space. In that case, Social City Movers is ready to assist you. We go above and beyond vacuuming and dusting and go deeply into corners to clear your home of dust, move-out, and dirt. We'll scrub the insides of your stove and remove the refrigerator from the horrible food smells. Take your time with everything else related to moving, and leave the work of cleaning up to us.

Our highly trained and experienced cleaning staff provides:

  • Free cleaning quotes
  • No worries, worry-free guarantee
  • Modern methods and techniques
  • Customizable cleaning plans for your home
  • Cleaning after construction for new and renovated homes
  • Services for packing household items to ease the transition
