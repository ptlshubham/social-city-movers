Packing Services

What kind of packing services are you looking for?

Full Packing Services
It's better not to worry about packing. We'll send a group of professional movers and packers to your residence to load everything from the attic to the basement. We will remove any things you need before moving and then swiftly load them up for the moving day.

Partial Packing Services
If you're comfortable carrying some (or even all) of your belongings on your own but need some help, we can meet your needs for packing. Perhaps you'd like us to help you pack a lot of breakables or prepare storage spaces for your move. Whatever you require, we're glad to assist.

UnPacking Services:

Moving can be tiring, no matter if you have hired professionals to handle the heavy lifting. If you'd like help settling into your new house, such as unpacking boxes, we can take on the entire task or provide just some hours of assistance. It's up to you. Are you moving tomorrow, and you're not packing yet? Do not fret. Contact your moving director as quickly as possible regarding arranging packing services. We'll do our best to meet your needs, even at the last minute.

Why should you hire a Moving and Unpacking Assistance?

It would be wonderful to step into your new home without worrying about unpacking the endless number of boxes for moving and knowing exactly where all your belongings are. Our organizing team helps you settle into your new residence as fast as we can. We also create an organizational system designed to meet your needs and lifestyle.
Here are some ways we can help you with your move:

  • Clean out your home before moving and drop off donations to your chosen charity or items that will be sold via consignment.
  • Set up appointments for other providers of services (e.g. moving companies, closet installers, moving companies, etc.)
  • Day-of-moving control and coordination with moving companies to ensure everything is packed carefully and then moved to the correct rooms of your new home.
  • Partially unpack your home and settle in your most important areas.
  • Complete home unpacking, organizing, and setting up systems.
  • The organizing tools from the Source are perfect for your new home.
  • Check-in for up to two weeks following your relocation to ensure the organizing system is effective for you and your family.
